

PROJECT IDENTITY
Design a complete Figma UI system for "HZ Hardware & Paints" — tagline: "Strong Foundations. Perfect Finishes." — a modern, high-trust Indian hardware retail brand serving homeowners, contractors, and builders.


DESIGN LANGUAGE
Industrial Modern + Indian Bazaar Precision. The brand sits where a Tier-1 Indian retail chain meets a global tool brand — think Croma meets Stanley Black & Decker. Clean, organised, grid-heavy layout with subtle desi warmth: saffron accents, a hint of geometric rangoli in dividers, and the trust cues of a neighbourhood hardware specialist scaled up digitally. No clutter. Maximum clarity.

INDIAN MODERN TOUCH — DESIGN DETAILS
1. GEOMETRIC ACCENT PATTERNS: Use a subtle grid-dot or small geometric tile (inspired by rangoli / Mughal jaali) as a 3–5% opacity background watermark in the hero section footer and about section. SVG pattern tile, not raster.

2. SAFFRON ACCENT STRIP: A 4px horizontal saffron (#FF8F00) stripe at the very top of the page (above the nav) — like the national flag header seen on government and trusted Indian retail sites. Reinforces local trust.

3. BILINGUAL MICRO-LABELS: Category labels carry a tiny Hindi subtitle in Noto Sans Devanagari 10px below the English name. Example: "Paints / पेंट्स", "Power Tools / पावर टूल्स". Only in category cards — nowhere else.

4. FESTIVE SALE RIBBON: A diagonal "Sale / सेल" ribbon badge on product cards — saffron background, white bold text, 45° rotation — for Diwali, Holi, or Independence Day campaigns. Design as a component variant (default hidden, festive active).

5. TRUST BADGES ROW: A horizontal strip below the hero with icons: "ISI Marked Products", "GST Invoice Available", "Free Delivery Above ₹999", "10,000+ Products in Stock". Use ₹ rupee symbol, not $. Indian trust signals.

6. PRICING FORMAT: All prices shown as ₹ (Indian Rupee). Format: ₹1,299 (no decimals for round numbers). Include MRP strikethrough + selling price for product cards. Tax note: "+ 18% GST" in 11px grey.

7. WHATSAPP-FIRST CONTACT: WhatsApp is the primary contact method for Indian users. Design a prominent WhatsApp CTA (green #25D366) that is more prominent than the email form. Include a "Chat on WhatsApp" button in the nav, hero, and as a floating sticky button on mobile.

8. PINCODE CHECK WIDGET: A small "Check Delivery to Your Pincode" input in the hero or product section — very common in Indian e-commerce. Input field + "Check" blue button, 40px height.

9. REGIONAL TEXTURE ACCENT: In the "Why Choose Us" section background, use a very faint corrugated metal / grid texture pattern (SVG, 4% opacity) — references the look of Indian hardware store shutters and warehouses.

10. CONTRACTOR LOYALTY BADGE: A "Verified Contractor" tag component — dark charcoal background, orange border, hardhat icon — for contractor-facing CTAs like "Bulk Supply" and "Contractor Support".
